Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 30, 2026  ·  The Ruff Society LLC  ·  New York, NY

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes — ever. Your data belongs to you.

1. Who We Are

Ruff Society Health is a pet health tracking application operated by The Ruff Society LLC, a limited liability company registered in the State of New York ("we," "us," or "our"). You can reach us at info@theruffsociety.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Ruff Society Health app at theruffsocietyhealth.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Profile photo: An optional photo you choose to upload for your profile.
  • Pet information: Your pet's name, breed, species, birthday, weight, sex, microchip number, and photo.
  • Health records: Vaccine records, vet visit notes, medications, symptoms, weight logs, feeding logs, and any notes or journal entries you create.
  • Walk data: Walk duration, distance, notes, and photos you take during walks.
  • Contacts: Vet, groomer, and caregiver contact information you choose to save.
  • Calendar events: Appointments and reminders you create in the app.

Information Collected Automatically

  • GPS location data: Your precise location is collected only during an active walk session when you start GPS tracking. Location data is used solely to draw your walk route and calculate distance. We do not track your location in the background or at any other time.
  • Session data: We store an authentication token in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in between sessions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Ruff Society Health app. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your pets' health records, walks, and notes
  • Send password reset emails when you request them
  • Display alerts for upcoming vaccine due dates and calendar events
  • Calculate walk statistics such as distance and pace
  • Respond to support inquiries you send us

We do not use your information to serve advertisements, build marketing profiles, or sell data to any third party.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider that uses industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit). Each user's data is isolated using Row Level Security — meaning your data can only be accessed by you when you are logged in with your account credentials.

Photos and files you upload are stored in secure cloud storage with access controls that prevent other users from accessing your files.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We work with the following service providers solely to operate the app. They process your data on our behalf under their own privacy policies and are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database, authentication, and file storage
  • GitHub Pages (github.com) — app hosting
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — map tiles displayed during walk tracking (your location is not transmitted to OpenStreetMap)

We do not share your information with any other third parties, including advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies.

6. GPS and Location Data

Location access is requested only when you actively start a walk. Your GPS coordinates are used to draw your walk route on the map and calculate distance. This data is stored in your account so you can view past walks. You can delete any walk record at any time, which permanently removes the associated location data. You may also deny location access in your device settings, in which case walk tracking features will not be available but all other app features will work normally.

7. Children's Privacy

Ruff Society Health is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info@theruffsociety.com and we will delete that information promptly.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all data stored in your account at any time within the app.
  • Correction: You can update your profile, pet information, and any records directly in the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual records (walks, vaccines, symptoms, etc.) within the app. To request full account deletion including all associated data, email us at info@theruffsociety.com and we will permanently delete your account within 30 days.
  • Data portability: You may request a copy of your data by emailing us.
  • Location: You can revoke GPS location permission in your device settings at any time.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your personal information, and opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@theruffsociety.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or by a prominent notice in the app. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Cookie & Data Notice
Last updated: March 30, 2026  ·  The Ruff Society LLC  ·  New York, NY

This notice explains what data Ruff Society Health stores in your browser and on your device, and why.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics tools. We store only what is necessary to keep you logged in and make the app work.

1. What Is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored in your browser by a website or app. Ruff Society Health uses browser local storage (similar to cookies) to remember your login session so you don't have to sign in every time you open the app.

2. What We Store and Why

Ruff Society Health stores the following data in your browser's local storage:

  • Authentication token — A secure token provided by our authentication system (Supabase) that keeps you logged in. This token expires automatically and is deleted when you sign out. It contains no personal information — it is simply a key that lets you access your account securely.
  • Session data — Basic session information required to maintain your login state between page visits.

We do not store:

  • Advertising or marketing cookies
  • Third-party tracking pixels
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Any data from other websites you visit

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may load resources in your browser:

  • Supabase — handles authentication and data storage. Supabase processes your login credentials securely and returns an authentication token stored locally in your browser.
  • OpenStreetMap via Leaflet.js — provides map tiles displayed when you use the walk tracking feature. Map tiles are loaded from OpenStreetMap's servers. Your GPS location coordinates are not sent to OpenStreetMap — they are processed entirely within the app in your browser.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the App files. GitHub may log standard server access data (IP address, browser type) for security and operational purposes per their own privacy policy.

4. How to Control or Delete Stored Data

You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove the authentication token. This will log you out of the app. Here's how:

  • iPhone/iPad (Safari): Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data → select "Cookies and other site data"
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari (Mac): Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All

Clearing browser data removes your local session but does not delete your account or any data stored in the app. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us at info@theruffsociety.com.

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" setting. Because we do not engage in cross-site tracking, behavioral advertising, or third-party analytics, this setting has no practical effect on your experience with our App.

6. Changes to This Notice

If we change what data we store or how we use it, we will update this notice and the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
